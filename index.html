The Online Data Validator reduces errors, lowers costs, and improves the accuracy of data

An accessible, efficient and easy to use data submission and validation tool, easily tailored to any government data set and reporting requirement. The Validator is developed and operated by Strategic Data Pty Ltd under contract to the Department of Health. Its current users include the Australian Institute of Health and Welfare (AIHW) and the Australian Mental Health Outcomes and Classification Network (AMHOCN).

About the Online Data Validator

The Online Data Validator allows large datasets to be examined for validity against preset rules, identifying data errors and anomalies and reducing the work required by data submitters and reviewers.

Features

  • easy comparison between different datasets and across multiple years
  • user-friendly insight into historical trends and recurring data issues
  • chat facility for facilitating and recording collaborative review of issues between submitters and reviewers
  • suite of reports that is comprehensive and customisable

Usable

Despite the detailed and complex workflow supported by the Validator it provides a light weight, intuitive interface to users.

Accessible

The application is compliant with WCAG 2.0 guidelines at the AA level.

Secure

The Online Data Validator employs security systems and protocols that satisfy the requirements of the Australian Signals Directorate Information Security Manual.
